About Us






we are the perfect clinic service



Outside of his medical practice, Dr. Rajkumar Sonone is passionate about promoting overall wellness and a balanced lifestyle. He enjoys spending time in nature, finding solace and inspiration in hiking and outdoor activities. His love for nature not only rejuvenates him but also reinforces his belief in the importance of holistic health. Dr. Sonone is an avid reader of both medical literature and literature on personal development, continuously seeking to expand his knowledge and integrate new insights into his practice. He also dedicates time to volunteering in community health initiatives, believing in giving back and supporting health education in underserved communities.
